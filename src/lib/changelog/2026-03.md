#### Card trays

Added a new tray type for playing cards that support standard card sizes (Standard, Mini American, Mini European, Euro, Japanese, Tarot, and Square). Cards rest on a sloped floor for easy retrieval with a finger cutout. Custom card sizes can be defined and are shared globally across all trays. [#15](https://github.com/Siege-Perilous/counterslayer/pull/15)
